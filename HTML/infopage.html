How to Play

Welcome to the game! Here's how to play:

- Click the button to earn coins.
- Every click earns you 1 coin.
- When you've earned enough coins, you can spend them
to upgrade your button.

- When using the ultra upgrade, it increases
the amount of coins you get by 25
